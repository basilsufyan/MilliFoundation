Introduction to Milli Foundation

Milli Foundation is a success oriented welfare and educational institution which is established for the purpose and in an effort to provide help and assistance in all parts of life to benefit the Muslim society. Hence, it is engaged to the best capability in establishment of Madrasas and Maktabas in different places to eliminate people from ignorance, misguidance, straying and unreligious practices. Furthermore, together with the prosperous class of the society, taking practical measures and steps by organizing religious, educational and training programs to pave way for educational and economic development for people suffering of misery, educational and economic backwardness. The Foundation is altogether struggling for the formation of an Islamic Society to maintain brotherhood, honor and justice to lead a prosperous life filled with peace and therefore establishing Fatwa centers, reconciliation committees to cooperate each-other in the society. Demonstrating significant performance in areas like education, health and welfare the Foundation is also engaged in offering all possible services to the oppressed humanity and marginalized groups of the society. The founder and general secretary of this institution is Mufti Saifur Rahman who is a talented scholar as well as a keen observer of current affairs and having the ability to set up frameworks for the surety of a better future for the community.

Our Vision and Mission

Main Objectives

  1. Promoting purposeful and quality education both modern and religious.
  2. Arranging scholarships for poor and deserving students.
  3. Arranging hospitals to facilitate basic health care for deprived people.
  4. Granting education& training support and sponsoring orphans & destitute children.
  5. Providing rehabilitation assistance to victims of natural disaster.
  6. Facilitating helpless people and families and cooperating them to the extent.
  7. Organizing seminars, conferences and symposiums from time to time for the betterment of the society.
  8. Generating feelings of mutual love, unity and brotherhood among different school of thought & sects.
  9. Publishing magazines and books in different languages as required by occasion.
  10. Promoting commerce and small industries for economic development.

Milli Foundation in the mirror of its services: Deeniyat Examination

Indecency is becoming common now days in the new generation who are obtaining modern education, innovation is being poisoned by contaminating their thinking and people impressed with the western thoughts started thinking success and perfection lies in adapting the western lifestyle. To deal with these sorts of hazardous situations and to promote religious education and training in toddlers, Milli Foundation organizes Religious Assessment annually in which participants scoring good marks are being honored with prizes in form of money and gift items where participants demonstrate exceptional interest.

Supporting helpless people and families

The Milli Foundation not only considers its duty to support financially and contribute helpless people & families even widowed & divorced women, but the most essential obligation of the utmost necessities of the society by offering its role in the actual fieldwork.

Rehabilitation to victims of Natural Disasters

The Milli Foundation has always been in the forefront providing rehabilitation assistance and restoration to victims of natural disaster by engaging in arrangements of food, cloth, shelter, medicine and essential necessities.

Eid Gifts and Ration Scheme

The Milli Foundation provides ration and Eid Gifts for poor people suffering of inflation and unemployment on the occasions of both Eids. Eid-ul-Fitr gift pack contains vermicelli (Sewai - sweets), cloths, and other vital items, while Eid-ul-Adha gift pack contains meat, spices and etc.
